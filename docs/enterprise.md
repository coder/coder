## Adding your license key

### Requirements

- Your license key
- Coder CLI installed

### Instructions

1. Save your license key to disk and make note of the path
2. Open a terminal
3. Ensure you are logged into your Coder deployment

   `coder login <access url>`

4. Run

   `coder licenses add -f <path to your license key>`
